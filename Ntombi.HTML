What is GirlCode?

GirlCode was founded in 2014 as a women-only hackathon to address the inequality and gender gap in the tech space.

GirlCode has evolved into an educational institution that engages women in tech, facilitates their skills advancement, and supports their entry into the tech industry.

GirlCode is more than just a bootcamp; it is a community built to empower women and non-binary individuals through tech education.
